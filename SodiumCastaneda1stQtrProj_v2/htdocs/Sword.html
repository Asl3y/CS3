Genshin Impact's primary melee weapons are swords. It can deal with two types of attacks. Its standard attack may chain up to 5 chain attacks, however a charged attack takes a little longer to charge before dealing a more powerful damaging blow. The nature of these charged strikes varies. They can cause swinging, uppercut slashing, and lunging, as well as potentially throwing adversaries back and creating a space between the character who dealt the charge attack and the opponent.
