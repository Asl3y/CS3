Choose Your Weapon, Defeat Bosses, Easier Combat

Sword

Swords are light and usually held in one hand; they are a balanced weapon capable of inflicting light and fast attacks as well as a reasonable amount of damage.

Claymore

Claymores are hefty weapons that are typically wielded with two hands; they are a larger form of the ordinary sword that can deal with heavy and lethal damage despite restricting the player's movements.

Catalyst

The catalyst is a weapon that generates basic elemental magical strikes; it is both lethal and versatile, and it may be used at any range.

Bow

Although it is not as powerful as the other weapons, the bow is nevertheless a force to be reckoned with because it can provide swift and constant strikes that can overwhelm adversaries.

Polearm

Polearms are often handled with one or two hands. They are an excellent weapon for fighters who want to inflict harm and poke their opponents from a safe distance while maintaining a safe distance from hostile strikes.
