The Claymore is a much larger weapon that causes far more damage than other melee weapon types. Their attacks take longer to deal, but each swing and assault deals more damage. This is very useful when dealing with difficult items like rocks in the environment and geo. Characters can use their claymore to smash down rock walls, revealing secret riches and places. They have a regular attack and an ultimate charge attack, which requires a brief delay before dealing a greater, more destructive claymore smash. Because of their size and weight, these regular attacks have a large swing radius a nd do not deal as quickly as other melee weapons. Their swing attacks can still be strung together to form a speedier battle sequence. Charge attacks momentarily deplete a character's stamina. Characters will begin to twist and swing as they charge their attacks. The duration of this attack sequence culminates with a final finishing swing at the conclusion of the stamina meter.
