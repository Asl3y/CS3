Catalysts are classified as a different type of weapon than conventional melee and ranged weapons. Rather of physical attacks, these deal elemental damage and magical attacks. The type of damage done depends on which character has it equipped and the Vision, which offers a character elemental abilities. A character's aspect will be vital to note in this scenario. Even Catalyst users are vulnerable to two sorts of assaults. Because of their organic nature, these assaults differ from character to character. These attacks are more typically observed as elemental orbs or beams, and when charged, they may have a greater attack area. When the attack is dealt with, its radius is seen.
