Polearms are spear-like weapons with a greater range than combat blades. Chained Polearm attacks feature varied outputs and actions such as sweeping, swinging, slashing, uppercut slashing, stabbing, and lunging. They move rapidly and quickly, dealing modest damage. Charged strikes can also cause characters to lunge forward, deal a massive powerful slash, or summon spears in a directional line. In addition to their attack kinds, they can infuse elemental damage, which changes the sequencing of their assaults when they are dealt. To attack, use the usual attack button, quick press to chain, and hold to deal a charged attack.
