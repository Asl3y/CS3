During combat, bow users can utilize one of two types of assaults. Normal attacks are the basic attacks that are dealt with on a regular basis and can be chained into 5 continuous mi-s range attacks. These chain blows can be utilized on multiple opponents in the vicinity. These strikes are likewise affected by character ranking, with 5-star characters extending the chain to 6 hits. Charged Attack: These attacks demand a brief pause before dealing with their more powerful strike. It takes much longer to prepare than a conventional strike, but it delivers more precision, damage, and extends the range of the arrow when released. To deal a critical hit, assault an adversary weak spot or use a charged attack to land a headshot with a bow. When dealt with, critical hit damage deals a higher proportion of damage.
